Privacy Policy

Hearth Board is designed to help people coordinate schedules and plan together.

Information we collect

We collect only basic account information such as your email address and the availability data you choose to place into shared boards. We do not collect anything else such as real names, addresses, etc and Hearth Board is currently only in a free to use state, so no credit card or payment information is required.

How we use information

Your information is used only to operate Hearth Board and provide the basic features of the platform.

Third-party services

Hearth Board uses trusted third-party providers including Supabase, Vercel and Resend to operate.

Data sharing

Hearth Board does not sell or pass on ANY your personal information.

Account deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.
